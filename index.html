About Us

89.1 Bicol Idol FM is the hottest radio station on the rise at live broadcast on our dial at 89.1 FM in Legazpi City, Albay. We pride ourselves and please our valued listeners with programs on latest news, unbiased public affairs program and queuing wide variety of music both old and new. BEST VARIETY, COOLEST HITS. 89.1 Bicol Idol FM offers a space for you to enjoy new and all-time favorite music.

BACKGROUND

Collective in-depth brainstorming sessions gave birth to 89.1 BICOL IDOL FM which is envisioned to be an epitome of a pro-active and socially responsible broadcast journalism outfit and setting a strategic direction of its integrative broadcasting activities geared towards an outcome-based and balance program format. Likewise, 89.1 IDOL FM aims to be the flagship in helping local business entrepreneurs through timely updates on the latest business trends and provide an avenue for them to listen, earn and invest. more

89.1 BICOL IDOL FM

89.1 BICOL IDOL FM is committed to achieve transformative goals based on quality standard system of operation through innovative, pro-active, informative and environment friendly approaches. It aims at delivering timely and balanced news and information, relevant agri-business trends, wholesome entertainment, public service and outreach programs. Broadcast operations are well within the framework and guiding principles of the KBP Standards Authority. more

BROADCAST OPERATIONS

89.1 BICOL IDOL FM is the only FM station in Bicol region that operates on a 10,000 watts power output starting 4:00 am and signs-off at 12:00 midnight. Our studio is located at the 2nd Floor, D7, Citispire Building, Imelda C. Roces Avenue, Gogon, Legazpi City. Our 10, 000 watts tower transmitter is located at Mt. Bariw, Barangay Estanza, Legazpi City. Our technical staff is composed of tried, tested and licensed broadcast engineers who have a wide exposure and experience in top broadcast stations in the locality. more
